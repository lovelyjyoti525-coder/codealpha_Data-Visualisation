# Hiring Trends & Job Market Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of hiring trends, job role distributions, and regional demand across the current job market. By processing raw recruitment data and visualizing it through dynamic dashboards, this project uncovers actionable insights into how top companies are expanding their workforces. 

**Author:** Jyoti Gupta

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** End-to-end data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing of raw job listings.
* **Power BI:** Dynamic data visualization, dashboard creation, and metric tracking.

## 📊 Dataset Highlights
The dataset comprises comprehensive recruitment metrics collected from various job listings:
* **99** Major Companies (including Amazon, Infosys, Google, Dell Technologies, and Reliance Retail)
* **283** Unique Job Roles
* **14** States & **49** Districts covered
* **88%** of the tracked companies actively hiring

## 💡 Key Insights & Findings
1. **Role Demand:** Human Resources (HR) and Talent Acquisition roles dominate the market, indicating heavy corporate investment in workforce expansion and recruitment infrastructure.
2. **Employment Types:** The market is heavily skewed toward long-term employment, with **82%** being Full-Time roles, **15%** Internships, and **3%** Part-Time or other arrangements.
3. **Geographical Concentration:** **58%** of the hiring is concentrated in major economic hubs. The top hiring states are Karnataka, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana, and Delhi.
4. **Market Consolidation:** A small cluster of top companies drives a massive portion of both hiring volume and overall generated revenue.

## 📂 Repository Structure

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Clone this repository to your local machine.
2. Open the `/Data` folder to review the transformed dataset in Excel.
3. Open the `.pbix` file in Power BI Desktop to interact with the hiring trends dashboard.
